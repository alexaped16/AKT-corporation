Our Process

"Old World" Skilled Labor

Here at AKT, we strive to be the best. We have been leaders in the industry since 1978. We specialize in producing high-quality reflectors and delineators. We supply highway contractors, departments of transportation, and any other highway safety companies looking for reflectors, delineators, and reflective products for their highway safety needs.Whether its anything from guardrail delineators to traffic signal lenses and everything in between, AKT assures its customers they will receive the highest quality products and the best customer service. All of our products are proudly made and assembled in America.

All of our plastic products come from our injection molding division Elgin Molded Plastics, a custom injection molder that has produced top-quality products since 1949. Elgin Molded Plastics has performed all kinds of custom molding jobs and can handle jobs of any size, and are always looking to add new business. Their facility is equipped with injection molding presses ranging in size from 85 tons to 760 tons, equipped with wide platens, and robots.

We have another division Empco-Lite that specializes mainly in construction lighting along with other industries such as marine lighting, airport lighting, and railroad lighting. Empco-Lite has been an industry leader since the 1960s and is constantly improving their product line to suit their customers needs.

For all you reflector and delineator needs choose AKT.
