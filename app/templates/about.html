About AKT Corporation

AKT Corporation was founded in 1978 as a manufacturer and supplier of delineators, guardrail delineators, barrier wall markers, and traffic signal lenses. AKT ships its products across the globe. Most of our products are used by Department of Transportations and highway contractors.
